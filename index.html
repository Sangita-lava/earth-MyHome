About the Universe

The universe is everything that exists, including all of space, time, matter, and energy. It contains countless galaxies, each with billions of stars, and the vast, largely empty spaces between them. The universe is constantly expanding, and it is believed to have originated from the Big Bang approximately 13.8 billion years ago. While we can observe a portion of it, a significant amount of the universe remains unseen, including dark matter and dark energy.
